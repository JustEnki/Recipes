Salmon Ingredients
1 tablesppon - paprika
1 teaspoon - cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon - kosher salt
1 teaspoon - fresh black pepper

2 teaspoons - thyme, fresh: Chopped

4 4 ounce - center-cut salmon fillets

1 tablespoon - vegetable oil

Salmon Steps
1. Combine paprika, thyme, cayenne pepper and 1 tablespoon salt in a bowl.
2. Sprinkle both sides of the salmon with the dry rub.
3. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet and add salmon, cook until browned
    3 to 4 minutes each side
